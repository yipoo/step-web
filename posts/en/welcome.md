# Welcome to Step

> Every Step Counts

We're excited to announce the official launch of **Step**!

## Why We Created Step

In today's fast-paced world, we often:
- Set ambitious goals but struggle to maintain them
- Download countless productivity tools that end up unused
- Get caught up in "check-in" culture and lose sight of our original goals

**Step was born from a simple insight:**

> People don't fail because they lack goals, but because they lack an execution method that fits their own rhythm.
